## What we deliver

- **Multi-activity platform** — parallel workshops (precision, coordination, communication) with **timed rotations**.
- **Multi-shooting challenge** — short relays, blindfolded formats or reversed instructions to train **trust** and **feedback**.
- **Cohesion paths** — collaborative goals, playful debriefs.
- **Archery** — accessible intro, **team** formats, visible scoring.
- **Giant sling** and **blowgun** — inclusive, quick to learn, high visual impact.
