# Technical training camps

**Arc IMPACT** runs **full-immersion camps**: meaningful shooting volume, clear technical progression and coaching from **state-certified** staff with deep experience in **field archery, 3D and national-team environments**. Most sessions take place at **La Féclaz** (Les Déserts, Savoie) with accommodation, classroom, gym, **Natur’Foam** course and easy access to the mountains for **half-day recovery** activities.

## 3D technical camp <span id="stage-3d"></span>

**Venue:** La Féclaz · **Length:** 1 week · **Intensity:** up to **5 h** shooting/day · **Group size:** **8** archers max.

Natur’Foam course, classroom, gym, permanent 3D. Hotel with **pool & sauna**. **Two half-days** for outdoor activities (seasonal). **Technical T-shirt** included.

- **July** — typical balance **5%** physical / **75%** technical / **20%** mental: consolidation, season planning, varied shooting contexts.
- **August** — **5%** physical / **55%** technical / **40%** mental: sharpening before a major goal, match routines.

## Indoor camp <span id="stage-indoor"></span>

Mental and **competition** work at **18 m** (**FFTA** members). Recurve, compound, barebow. **4–6 h** shooting/day. Indicative split: **10%** physical / **60%** technical / **30%** mental—video blocks, match scripts, stress control.

**[Ask for the next indoor block →](../contact/)**

## Field / outdoor (TAE) <span id="stage-tae"></span>

**70 / 60 / 50 m** (recurve/compound) and **30 m** barebow. **4–5 h** / day. FFTA-style rotations between field and covered structure.
