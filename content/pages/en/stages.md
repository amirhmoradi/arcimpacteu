# Technical training camps

Most camps run at **La Féclaz** (Les Déserts, Savoie) with accommodation, classroom, gym and 3D course.

## 3D technical camp <span id="stage-3d"></span>

**Venue:** La Féclaz · **Duration:** 1 week · **Up to** 5 h shooting/day · **Group size:** 8.

Natur'Foam course, theory room, gym, permanent 3D. Hotel with pool & sauna. Two half-days for outdoor activities. Technical T-shirt included.

## Indoor camp <span id="stage-indoor"></span>

Mental & competition focus at **18 m** (FFTA members). Recurve, compound, barebow. **4–6 h** / day.

## Field / outdoor (TAE) <span id="stage-tae"></span>

**70 / 60 / 50 m** (recurve/compound) and **30 m** barebow. **4–5 h** / day.

## Prestige 3D <span id="stage-prestige"></span>

**Roche-sur-Linotte** estate — up to 8 archers, 50+ 3D targets.

## Franco-Swiss youth 3D <span id="stage-ados"></span>

Youth · **Bauges** mountains · tipi village · **3 days**.

## Physical preparation <span id="stage-physique"></span>

Winter block focused on strength, core and cardio for all bow types.

## Competition coaching <span id="stage-coaching"></span>

Theory plus on-line coaching for all equipment categories.

## Clubs & regions <span id="stage-clubs"></span>

On request — weekends or 4-day blocks.
